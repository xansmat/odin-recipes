Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish made with noodles, meat sauce, and a cheese mixture

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Sweet red sauce
  • Hotdogs
  • Evaporated milk
  • Flour, butter and cheese
  1. Cook the beef
  2. Simmer with sauce
  3. Layer the sauce, boiled noodles, and the milk and cheese
  4. Bake until golden
